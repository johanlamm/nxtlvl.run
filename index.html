About Us

nxtlvl.run was created by Leo Magnusson, elite runner (28:45 10km) and second European over 3000m steeplechase 2024, and Johan Lamm, software engineer, former middle distance runner and nowadays aspiring distance runner.

The idea was born at a training camp, inspired by the need for affordable, personalized plans. We saw how many runners were paying outrageous prices for copy-pasted, one-size-fits-all programs that failed to consider individual needs. nxtlvl.run was built to change that.
